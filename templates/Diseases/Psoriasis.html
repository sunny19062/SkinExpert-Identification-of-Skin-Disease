Psoriasis

Overview

Psoriasis is a chronic autoimmune skin condition that speeds up the growth cycle of skin cells, causing them to build up rapidly on the surface of the skin. It is characterized by red, itchy, and scaly patches that may be painful or bleed.

Symptoms

  • Red patches of skin covered with silvery scales
  • Dry, cracked skin that may bleed
  • Itching, burning or soreness
  • Thickened, pitted or ridged nails

Treatment

Treatment options include topical creams and ointments, light therapy, and oral or injectable medications.
