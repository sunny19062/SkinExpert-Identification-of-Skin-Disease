Warts

Overview

Warts are small, rough growths on the skin that are caused by a viral infection. They are most commonly found on the hands, feet, and face. Warts can be contagious and spread from person to person or from one part of the body to another. Treatments include over-the-counter medications, freezing, burning, or cutting off the wart, or prescription medication.

Symptoms

  • Small, rough growths on the skin
  • Can be raised or flat
  • May have tiny black dots in the center

Treatment

Treatments include over-the-counter medications, freezing, burning, or cutting off the wart, or prescription medication.
