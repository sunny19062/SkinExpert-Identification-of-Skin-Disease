Herpes STDs

Overview

Herpes is a common sexually transmitted infection (STI) caused by the herpes simplex virus (HSV). It can cause sores on the genitals, mouth, or rectum, and can also cause symptoms such as fever, body aches, and swollen lymph nodes. Herpes is most often spread through sexual contact, but it can also be spread through skin-to-skin contact.

Symptoms

  • Sores on the genitals, mouth, or rectum
  • Fever
  • Body aches
  • Swollen lymph nodes

Treatment

There is no cure for herpes, but antiviral medications can help manage outbreaks and reduce the risk of transmission to others.
