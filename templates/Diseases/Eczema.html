Eczema

Overview

Eczema is a skin condition characterized by itchy, dry, red, and inflamed skin. It is common in children but can occur in adults as well. Symptoms can be triggered by a variety of factors including stress, allergens, and irritants. Treatments include over-the-counter creams and ointments, as well as prescription medications.

Symptoms

  • itchiness
  • redness
  • dry skin
  • inflammation

Treatment

Treatments include over-the-counter creams and ointments, as well as prescription medications.
