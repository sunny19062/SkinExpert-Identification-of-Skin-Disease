Pyogenic Skin Infection

Overview

Pyogenic skin infections are bacterial infections of the skin that lead to the formation of pus. These infections can occur in any part of the body, and can be caused by different types of bacteria. Symptoms include redness, swelling, pain, and the formation of pus. Treatment involves antibiotics and sometimes drainage of the infected area.

Symptoms

  • Redness
  • Swelling
  • Pain
  • Pus formation

Treatment

Treatment involves antibiotics and sometimes drainage of the infected area.
