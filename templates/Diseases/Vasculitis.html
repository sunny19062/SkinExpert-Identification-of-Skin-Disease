Vasculitis

Overview

Vasculitis is a group of diseases that cause inflammation of blood vessels. This can lead to damage of the blood vessels and the organs they supply. Symptoms vary depending on the affected organs, and can include fever, fatigue, weight loss, skin rashes, and joint pain. Treatment depends on the type and severity of vasculitis, and may include medications to reduce inflammation and suppress the immune system.
