Seborrheic Dermatitis

Overview

Seborrheic dermatitis is a common skin condition that mainly affects the scalp, causing scaly patches, red skin and stubborn dandruff. It can also affect other oily areas of the body, such as the face, upper chest and back.

Symptoms

  • Redness
  • Greasy, swollen skin
  • Yellow or white flakes on the affected area
  • Itchiness

Treatment

Treatments for seborrheic dermatitis include medicated shampoos, topical corticosteroids and antifungal medications. In severe cases, oral medications may also be prescribed.
